1. Modify the programmer's instructions
2. You can REPLACE an instruction with a different one
3. You can DELETE an instruction that the programmer left
4. Change the SETTINGS OF THE ROBOT to modify its movement

There is no maximum amount of moves that you can change
Try to keep your changes subtle so that the programmer cannot easily figure out what you have changed
Forward speed & backward speed control how fast it goes, not how far it moves
Left/Right angle control how much it turns
Left/Right speed control how fast it turns
